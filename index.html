Welcome to Xi Bai's homepage

I am currently a software engineer working at the BBC Design & Engieering. Up until 2013 I was a postgraduate student under the supervision of David Robertson, Ewan Klein and Wamberto Vasconcelos at the University of Edinburgh. My doctoral thesis is focused on making the Web evolve into a decentralised environment where user agents can easily form online communities and share knowledge via specifications on reciprocal tasks. Around this work, my research interests are focused on Process-Calculus-Based Coordination, Semantic Annotation, Data Integration & Linked Data, P2P Social Networking and Agent-Oriented Programming.
