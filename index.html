Welcome to Xi Bai's homepage

My doctoral thesis is focused on making the Web evolve into a decentralised environment for user agents to easily form online community dedicated to knowledge sharing driven by specifications on tasks. Around this work, my research interests are focused on Process-Calculus-Based Coordination, Semantic Annotation, Data Integration & Linked Data, P2P Social Networking, and Agent-Oriented Programming. I was a postgraduate student under the supervision of David Robertson, Ewan Klein and Wamberto Vasconcelos.
